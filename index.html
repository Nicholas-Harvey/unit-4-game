Play the Crystal Guessing Game!

  • To play the game click the button. A score will be generated and displayed.
  • Then, click on a Crystal: this will generate a random number which will be added to your total score. Each crystal will have a different random value
  • The object of the game is to get your score to equal the score generated at the begining of each game. Once you win or loose, click the button again to restart the game!
